2025 Stack Overflow Survey Results & Visualization

The Stack Overflow Developer Survey is an annual survey that gets information on developers from around the world. Results are available to the public. This dashboard was created using that data.

Audience

As mentioned in my Technical Report, this dashboard is for people who want an overview of developers' job satisfaction and type of work location throughout the world.

KPIs and Measures

  1. Responders' Age
  2. Responders' Location Type (Remote, In-Person, Hybrid, etc.)
  3. Responders' Work Experience
