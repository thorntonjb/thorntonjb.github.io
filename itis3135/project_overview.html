HISTORY OF EL CLÁSICO

PROJECT OVERVIEW

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to show the History of El Clásico - any matches between the rival teams FC Barcelona and Real Madrid. This will also give the client (information below) a chance to see their request.

Intended User

The intended user of this application is one who is interested in learning more about the World's Game and one of the fiercest rivalries to ever hit to global stage.

Content Overview

The history of football has always been based on the great rivalries. One of the greatest in the history of the sport is that of FC Barcelona and Real Madrid. The start of the rivalry stemmed from controversial referee calls and battles over player signatures.
