Visual Analytics, Project 3

Design Mastery Report

2025 Stack Overflow Survey Results

The Stack Overflow Developer Survey is an annual survey that gets information on developers from around the world. Results are available to the public. This dashboard was created using that data.

Audience & Intent

As mentioned in my Technical Report, this dashboard is for people who want an overview of developers' job satisfaction and type of work location throughout the world - now more accessible for people with colorblindness and more streamlined story-telling capabilities.

Story & Structure

The story I want to tell ties job satisfaction with work location. What kind of connections can be made for software developers' work location in Russia between age groups? This dashboard can help answer that question. The World Map at the bottom left of the dashboard provides the foundation for the entire dashboard. Upon clicking a country, the rest of the dashboard updates to narrow the scope of the story to individual countries.

Design Choices

I approached color with the intent of making this dashboard more accessible to people with colorblindness. To get rid of clutter, adding functionality for the World Map to affect the entire dashboard allowed me to get rid of some legends/external filters that I did not need. What perception or design principles guided these choices? Having more whitespace actually helps more aspects of a dashboard to stand out. I chose a completely different color set for the World Map to have it stand out from the other visuals and indicate that it has filtering capabilities for the dashboard.

Reflection

Working with Tableau Public Desktop was the greatest challenge for me. Eventually, I switched to the web version of Tableau Public to prevent any more technical issues with the dataset (which is very large) in the future. The KPIs that I decided to focus on work very well together and I believe my color choices between the three work well.
If I had more time, I would play around more with the colors and see what other KPIs I could integrate into the dashboard to give the most information while staying away from overstimulating my users.
