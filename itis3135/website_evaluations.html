ITIS3135 WEBSITE EVALS

Serious Business - great

Serious Business is an agency for startups and scaleups based in Europe. Their brand strategy, visual identity, and services are all made extremely clear on the website.

Contrast

  • Fantastic contrast throughout entire site
      • Contrast changes once a new menu is brought up to shift the readers' focus

Repetition

  • Animations while scrolling are consistent and satisfying
      • Colors are consistent - color theory is theorying
  • Animations on button clicks are consistently smooth

Alignment

  • Titles are centered and larged
      • Captions are under their respective image

Proximity

  • Items are spread out enough to be readable
      • Nothing is overwhelming about the site

Ling's Cars - not so great

Ling's Cars' website displays all the different types of Cars that Ling sells. There is a lot going on in every spot on the website. This makes it hard to navigate.

Contrast

  • It's there, but the screen is so busy, it makes contrast seem like a stranger

Repetition

  • Animations when hovering over cars are consistent
      • List of car brands on the left side is overdone...
  • Car titles are all the same font, color
  • Repeating things too much - also due to too many cars on the front page

Alignment

  • The entire page is within a single box, centered in the viewport
      • Business info section (orange gradient box) includes centered p elements :(
  • In fact, every time a p element is featured, it is centered.

Proximity

  • Everything is way too close to each other
      • Images are too close to each other, and too large due to many unnecessary captions
